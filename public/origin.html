Our Approach

Kairos means the right moment.

Most consultants focus on the application. I focus on the student. The application is just the expression of the work we do together.

01
Story First
Before we open Common App, we spend sessions excavating who you actually are — your values, your experiences, your thinking patterns. The essay doesn't create the story. The story creates the essay.
02
Strategic Precision
ED, ED2, EA, RD — timing is everything. School selection, major choice, application round, essay positioning: every decision is made with your specific profile in mind. No cookie-cutter lists.
03
Voice Preservation
I never write a single word of your essay. I coach, challenge, and refine — but every sentence is yours. Admissions officers can detect a ghost-written essay. Authenticity is your greatest competitive advantage.
04
Cultural Fluency
I understand international students — whether you're at an IB school in Singapore, a boarding school in the UK, or a private school in Seoul. I've worked with students from Korea, Jordan, Ethiopia, Singapore, the US, and more. I translate your experience into a language elite universities respond to.
