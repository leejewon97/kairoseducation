About

The person who will actually work with your child.

Ron Kim
Founder · Kairos Education

Ron Kim founded Kairos after years inside the admissions industry, including time as a consultant at Crimson Education, one of the largest firms in the world. He learned what the big firms do well. He also saw what they do to families: once you sign, you are often handed to a junior associate, while the person with the real track record stays a name on the website.

Kairos is built to be the opposite. Every session, every essay, every strategy call is Ron himself. He takes on only a small number of families each year, because that is the only way to know a student well enough to help them stand out.

Ron grew up in the international school system in Korea and studied at the University of Illinois. Over his career he has guided students into Harvard, Yale, Columbia, Penn, Dartmouth, Cornell, Duke, Johns Hopkins, and the University of Chicago, and has worked on the counseling side of the desk at international schools.

The students I remember are not the ones with the highest test scores. They are the ones who found their real story and had the courage to tell it.
Ron Kim · Founder

At Kairos, your child will never be handed to someone else. The strategy, the feedback, the calls: that is Ron, every time.

The Process

How it works.

A clear path from where your child is now to the acceptance letter. Every step is with Ron, in order, with nothing left to the last minute.

Assessment & positioning

Ron starts by understanding your child honestly: their record, their strengths, and what genuinely sets them apart. This becomes the foundation everything else is built on.

The strategy roadmap

A year-by-year plan across courses, activities, testing, and the application timeline, sequenced so the work is paced and deliberate.

Building a standout profile

Ron guides the research, leadership, and projects that turn a strong student into a memorable applicant, not just a longer list.

School list & application strategy

The right schools and the right rounds. Early decision, early action, and regular, chosen around your child's real chances.

Essays & interviews

Your child writes; Ron coaches and refines until every essay sounds like them at their best, then prepares them to walk into any interview calm.
