Our Approach

Kairos means the right moment.

Most consultants focus on the application. We focus on the student. The application is just the expression of the work we do together.

01
Story First
Before we open Common App, we spend sessions excavating who you actually are — your values, your experiences, your thinking patterns. The essay doesn't create the story. The story creates the essay.
02
Strategic Precision
ED, ED2, EA, RD — timing is everything. School selection, major choice, application round, essay positioning: every decision is made with your specific profile in mind. No cookie-cutter lists.
03
Voice Preservation
We never write a single word of your essay. We coach, challenge, and refine — but every sentence is yours. Admissions officers can detect a ghost-written essay. Authenticity is your greatest competitive advantage.
04
Cultural Fluency
We understand international students — whether you're at an IB school in Singapore, a boarding school in the UK, or a private school in Seoul. We've worked with students from Korea, Jordan, Ethiopia, Singapore, the US, and more. We translate your experience into a language elite universities respond to.

About

Built on real results,
not brand names.

I founded Kairos Education because I saw a gap: students who deserved elite outcomes were getting generic strategies. Big consulting firms scale by selling templates. I scale by knowing every student by name.

I graduated from an IB international school in Korea and went on to study Sociology and Criminology at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I then spent years as a college consultant, IB/AP instructor, and international school college counselor — working directly with students from Korea, Singapore, Ethiopia, Jordan, the UK, and the US, guiding them to admissions at Harvard, Yale, Columbia, UPenn, Dartmouth, Cornell, Duke, Johns Hopkins, and UChicago.

"The students I remember aren't the ones with the highest SAT scores. They're the ones who found their real story — and had the courage to tell it."

At Kairos, I work directly with every student. You will never be handed off to a junior coach. The strategy, the essay feedback, the calls — that's me, every time.
